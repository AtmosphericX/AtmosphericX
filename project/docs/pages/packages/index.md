# Internal Packages
---
AtmosphericX has a growing collection of internal and open source packages that are used across the project for various purposes, including event parsing, placefile generation, weather station data retrieval, and more. These packages are developed in house to meet the specific needs of the project and are often made available as open source for the community to use and contribute to.

See: [NPM](https://www.npmjs.com/~k3y0mi) for a comprehensive list of packages, their descriptions, and links to their respective documentation and repositories.
